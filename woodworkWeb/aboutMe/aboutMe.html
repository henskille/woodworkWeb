Henry Killen

Hello and welcome to my gallery. My name is Henry and I am a woodworker based in New York City. I got my undergraduate degree in Neuroscience from Reed College in 2020, and after graduating into the COVID pandemic I decided to move into a converted sprinter van full time. I am self taught in woodworking, design, off grid electrical systems, web design and more. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions about my work.
